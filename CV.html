Design Projects

University Capstone Project

Our capstone team works together with the Alberta Children’s Hospital Research Institute to create an application for a new hand-held DNA sequencer called the MinION. Our algorithm identifies human DNA in real-time – and then throws it out! What’s left is invasive (such as virus, or viral) DNA. This sample could be used to help scientists identify the strain and characteristics of a virus; resulting in information that can estimate the spread of the virus and expedite the development of a vaccine. In rural areas like Guinea after the outbreak of Ebola, this process took weeks, but with our solution, it could just be a matter of hours.
